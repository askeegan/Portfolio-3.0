03 : About Me

Who is Alejandro?

I am a Málaga-born and raised Full Stack Engineer, currently living near Düsseldorf, Germany. Besides learning German, my interest lie in the Fitness world, my wonderful family, and - of course - coding websites.


My Journey

After exploring different careers in Spain and England, I finally made the decision to do what I am truly passionate about: Creating outstanding websites that feature both: Modern functionalities and a contemporary design.


Education

I began my career with FreeCodeCamp, earning my first certificate in Responsive Web Design. Building on this foundation, I sought further knowledge through Codecademy where I completed certifications in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ultimately earned a Front-End Engineer qualification. Lastly, I enrolled in the DCI Institute to pursue my goal of becoming a full-stack developer. I am currently continuing my education there.
